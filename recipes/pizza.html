Deliciously crafted with the finest ingredients, this pizza is a perfect blend of crispy crust, melty cheese, and bold flavors. A slice of happiness in every bite!

Ingredients

Garlic Butter

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 pinch dried rosemary
  • 1 pinch salt

Alfredo Sauce

  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tablespoon salt
  • 1 dash ground black pepper
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3/4 grated Romano cheese

Pizza Dough

  • 1 .25 ounce package instant yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried reosemary
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour

Chicken

  • 2 boneless chicken breast halves, roasted
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1/4 dried thyme
  • 1/4 poultry seasoning
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
